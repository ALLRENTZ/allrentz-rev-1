Record everything visible before opening a product page, including name, image, capacity, type, model, location, rate, availability, and calls to action.

Record specifications, dimensions, applications, features, accessories, documents, certifications, branch information, availability, delivery, pricing, and RFQ behavior.

Document search behavior, filters, sorting, comparison tools, location logic, and whether results lead directly to an RFQ or reservation action.

List confusing, incomplete, duplicated, or vendor-specific patterns that should not become part of the ALLRENTZ model.
