## Anchors (do not re-litigate without cause)

- **Problem:** refinery/petrochemical procurement wastes hours per rental chasing COIs, OSHA certs, MSAs, and non-public rates.
- **Not:** a construction marketplace, a vendor-hiding broker, a "call for pricing" site, or a tool for general contractors.
- **Core moat:** the Compliance Vault — structural switching cost once MSAs, COIs, and certs are tracked in ALLRENTZ.
- **Model:** commission on gross rental value + Compliance Vault subscription + consolidated invoicing fee.
