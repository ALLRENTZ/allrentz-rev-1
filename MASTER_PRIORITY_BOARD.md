# CORE PRODUCT PRINCIPLES

## Operational First
The platform must solve real refinery, terminal, and industrial coordination problems before adding advanced automation.

## Trust Over Hype
No fake metrics.
No fake marketplace scale.
No misleading AI claims.
No fake customer logos.
No false operational data.

## Assist, Don't Replace
AI supports workflows.
Humans remain in control.

## Workflow Before Intelligence
Stable operational workflows come before advanced AI systems.

## Explainability Matters
Recommendations must always explain:
- why
- how
- operational benefit
- compliance impact

## Industrial Simplicity
The UI should feel:
- operational
- fast
- dependable
- clean
- high trust

NOT:
- flashy
- overdesigned
- startup gimmicky

## Build Operational Leverage
Goal:
Reduce:
- calls
- emails
- spreadsheets
- delays
- vendor confusion
- compliance chasing
